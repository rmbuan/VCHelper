TESTING POLICY:

  1. A score of zero will be recorded for a missed assignment or examination unless the student presents the teacher with official substantiation of the absence the first day s/he returns to class.
  2. To register a pass in this course, the student must achieve a final mark of 50 percent on the theory portion of the course and the practical portion of the course.
  3. In order to be credited for assignments the student MUST average AT LEAST 50% on EXAMINATIONS, other wise the student's test marks will become the course mark.
  4. All tests are marked as 40% theory and 60% practical. The combination of the two marks on a single test will detemine the mark for that particular test.

ASSIGNMENT POLICY:

  1. Students are responsible for reading the appropriate course material prior to the lecture.
  2. All assignments are due promptly at the beginning of the class on the due date. Assignments handed in during the class, for any reason, will be marked late. Assignments submitted after the class will NOT be marked. All assignmnets and projects submitted should adhere to the documentation standards of your teacher. The student is responsible for keeping a back up copy of each assignment submitted.
